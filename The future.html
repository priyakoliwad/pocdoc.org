Looking to the Future

Where will Pocket Doctor go next?

Inforamtional advances

For our next steps, we are looking to work with medical professionals on our infographics to ensure the most relevant information possible. In our app, we have infographics that run users through a decision chart to help them decide if they should seek medical attention or not. By working with medical profesionals, we can provide top quality information.

Languages

To expand our userbase, we will add more audio translations to explain our infographics.
Languages inclund: Spanish, Arabic, Portugese, Mandrin, and various African languages.

Beta Testing

To fully know how to best alter and improve our app, our forst next step will be to pair with organizations to beta test the app and see our project in action. Once we know our product is the best it can be, we will launch our app in the real world!

Technovation

After experiencing the software side to building Pocket Doctor, we wanted to learn more about the steps toward implementing our app into the real world. These steps would focus a lot more on the entrepreneurial side to technology. .
